  • What is {{localTitle}}?
  • {{localTitle}} is a tool to crowd-curate and perform data analysis on {{localActvityName}} videos. Users are able to submit videos for annotation and annotate videos themselves. Both videos and analytics will be sortable and filterable by various criteria, including annotations (i.e., by which moves are present in the video).

  • How do I earn reputation points?
  • Users can make {{localNumberOfCurrentAnnotationsNeeded}} annotations on a video . All annotations earn a user {{localNumberOfPointsToAwardForAnnotation}} points. Reputation points expire on a monthly basis.
  • Alternatively, users can purchase reputations points with a monthly subscription costing {{localMonthlyCost}}
  • Can I lose reputation points? How?
  • Yes, you can. Reputation points are lost in the following ways:
    1. Annotations made by a user are flagged as incorrect by {{localNumberOfFlagsAnAnnotationNeedsBeforeReptuationDeduction}} or more users. Such an event results in a loss of {{localNumberOfPointsToDeductForBadAnnotation}} reputation points
    2. User creates a move or rank or weight class name that is flagged by {{localNumberOfFlagsAnAnnotationNeedsBeforeReptuationDeduction}} other users as inappropriate
    3. User posts a video that is flagged by {{localNumberOfFlagsAnAnnotationNeedsBeforeReptuationDeduction}} other users as inappropriate
    4. User posts a video that is flagged as removed by {{localNumberOfFlagsAnAnnotationNeedsBeforeReptuationDeduction}} other users before being rated with a {{localMinimumAnnotationRatingAVideoFlaggedAsRemovedNeedsToPreventUserDeduction}} or higher
